Scratch Game Arcade

Colour Pop
Click as many bubbles of the chosen colour as you can before it switches!

In this game, we learned about:
  • Arrays
  • Variables
  • Nested repeat loops


Tennis Rallying
Hit the tennis ball back and forth with Jordyn! Use your left and right arrow keys to control your movement. When you miss, they get a point and vice versa. First to get to 3 points wins!


Pong
The paddle sprite is controlled by the player via key presses and moves left and right at the bottom of the screen attempting to keep a ball that bounces around from touching the ground.
